Being a sports person since school days, developed keen interest on Sports Medicine and took up the career in Medical field. Had won many medals and laurels in different sports including track & field events like short distance running, Shotput, Discus, etc., Team games like football, kabadi etc., and individual games like tennis.

After joining the medical school, had spontaneously developed interest towards Medical Research. After completing MBBS., took up the challenge and joined the field of Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation by Choice. Initially at Madras Medical College, I had gained adequate exposure and experience in the field overall but developed interest in Neuro, Rheumatology and Orthopedic Rehabilitation.

Later during Post Graduation, had opportunity to develop expertise in the field of Pediatric Rehabilitation, Infertility and Obesity related disorders, and has became the core area of Practice. Being in the field of Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation, the field itself covers the metabolic disorders management with wholistic approach.
